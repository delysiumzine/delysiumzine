ABOUT

What is a fanzine?

A fanzine is a publication that contains art, writing, and other work centered around a certain theme.

 

Will this zine be physical or digital?

After Death, Life Again will be released as a physical, A5 size zine, with a digital PDF available for download during our pre-order period. 


What is a split-profit zine? 

Any profit made will be divided between our contributors/mods and a reputable charity. Our charity will be determined at a later date, but it will be related to providing aid for Palestine. The receipt from the donation will be posted to our social media once sales end.

When will preorders open? 

January 17 - February 28.


Where will the zine ship from?

We have shipping mods based in the US and France, and will be able to ship to a large range of countries. Due to carrier restrictions, we cannot ship to the following locations: Russia, Belarus, Yemen, Syria, North Korea, Afghanistan, Somalia, and Israel.


Will group orders be available?

Please email us if you are interested in personally hosting a GO! However, the mods are not responsible for items once they have been shipped to organizers, so please ensure you are signing up for GOs that you trust.
