DISCO ELYSIUM ZINE: PREORDERS OPEN!

“After Death, Life Again” is an unofficial Disco Elysium fanzine exploring the past, present, and futures of Revachol.


Proceeds will be split between our contributors and Anera, a charity supporting refugees and those hurt by conflicts in Palestine, Lebanon, and Jordan.

Preorders for the zine are OPEN from January 31 - March 14, with shipping taking place in May/June 2025.


The zine is a physical, 120 page, A5 book, with digital and physical merch options available, featuring work from 40 talented artists & writers.

ABOUT

What will the fanzine look like?

"After Death, Life Again" will be a physical, 120 page, A5 zine, with a digital PDF available for download after our preorder period. It will include artwork, writing, and merch items from our 40 talented contributors.


Where will the profits go?

Our proceeds will be evenly shared between our contributors/mods and and Anera, a charity supporting refugees and those hurt by conflicts in Palestine, Lebanon, and Jordan.


How long will preorders be open?

Our preorder period is from January 31 - March 14, 2025. Any extensions to this date will be announced on our socials.

Where will the zine ship from?

We will be shipping from the East Coast of the United States, and will be able to ship to a large range of countries. All packages will be sent with a tracking number and insurance, included with shipping.


Can I make a group order?

Please email us if you are interested in personally hosting a group order! We are not responsible for items once they have been shipped to organizers, so please ensure you are signing up for GOs that you trust.

COMMUNITY COPIES

What are community copies?

We recognize that not every person interested in the zine may be able to afford a copy. However, we also aim to compensate our contributors, whose work will bring this project to life, and generate a substantial amount of money to donate to Anera.


In hopes of increasing the availability of the physical zine, we have a tip jar open on Ko-fi for customers who would like to donate to fund community copies of the zine for others.


Who will receive the copies?

After POs close, we will enter everyone who submitted a request form into a raffle, and selected entries will receive a physical zine. We will email everyone who signed up to inform them whether they have received a community copy or not. Any leftover funds from community copy tips will be donated to Anera.
